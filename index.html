Headphone

Headphones are essential audio devices used for personal listening, offering an immersive experience by delivering high-quality sound directly to the ears. Available in various styles—such as over-ear, on-ear, and in-ear—they cater to different preferences in comfort and sound isolation. Modern headphones come equipped with advanced features like noise cancellation, wireless Bluetooth connectivity, and long battery life

Speaker

Speakers are devices designed to produce high-quality sound for a wide range of audio experiences, from music to movies and presentations. Available in various sizes and configurations, speakers can range from compact portable units to large, multi-channel systems for home theaters. Modern speakers often feature wireless connectivity through Bluetooth or Wi-Fi, allowing for easy integration with smartphones, laptops, and other devices
